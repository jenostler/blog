About Me

Hi, there! My name is Jenna Ostler, and I'm glad that you're here! I created this site as a way to share my thoughts, ideas, and interests. Allow me to introduce myself and give you a look into who I am.

I grew up as an Air Force brat, so that means I have lots of different answers for the "Where are you from?" question. A few years ago, I moved from California (northern ... we're not talking beaches and palm trees, here) to Provo, Utah for school.

I graduated in 2016 with a Bachelor of Arts degree in Interdisciplinary Humanities. (This is where most people say, "...in what?") Basically, I spent four amazing years studying cultures around the globe and the ways people have expressed themselves throughout history. If you ever want to take an hour or two to analyze a painting, talk about sacred Mayan rituals, or theorize ... well, pretty much anything, I'm your girl.

I've been married to the man of my dreams for 3 blissful years. David is my best friend, biggest fan, and forever crush. We love spending time together, whether that be while getting hooked on new TV shows, coding and designing websites, working out, trying new recipes, or making each other laugh until our stomachs burst.

As you take a closer look into my life and the things that are on my mind, I hope that you will read things that make you laugh, think, feel, and maybe even encourage you to change or try something new.

So, ready, set, JO! Read away!
